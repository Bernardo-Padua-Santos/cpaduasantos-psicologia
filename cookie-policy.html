Cookie Policy

This website uses cookies exclusively for statistical purposes, in order to improve your browsing experience.

What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. These files help us understand how the site is used.

Which cookies do we use?

We use Google Analytics to collect anonymous information about user behavior, such as the most visited pages and average time on site.

How can you manage cookies?

You can accept or reject cookies using the consent banner displayed on your first visit. You can also delete or block cookies at any time via your browser settings.

Consent

Cookies will only be activated after your explicit consent. Without it, Google Analytics will not load.

For more information, please see our Privacy Policy.
